This is a 3D visualization of female adult brown bear energetics from Alaska as provided here.

The bears are scaled based on their net body mass and colored based on the bear population group they are from.

GAAR: brown. KATM: blue. KOD: pink. LACL: green.

CONTROLS: A + D: Move left or right. W + S: Move up or down. Q + E: Zoom in or out. Mouse drag: Rotate bears.
